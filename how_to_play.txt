Asteroids (1979) clone by Sophia Al-Nawasreh

arrow keys to move around
spacebar to shoot bullets
t to teleport to a random location on the screen

move around to dodge the asteroids and shoot them to make them smaller. clear all the asteroids to progress and increase the difficulty
you will accelerate if you hold the controls, so move wisely to avoid crashing into an asteroid
your bullets will wrap around the screen up to a certain distance
your ship can also wraparound

enemy spaceship will spawn when score is divisible by 150
